Mangata and Gallo is an Austin-based jewelry company specializing in meaningful moments and milestones. Founded by designer and diamond specialist Mariana Gallo, our jewelry store crafts exquisite engagement rings, wedding bands, and anniversary gifts to commemorate life's most precious memories.

Our classic yet contemporary jewelry selection focuses on timeless elegance and unparalleled quality. Each piece is meticulously designed in our Austin atelier using ethically sourced diamonds and precious metals. Whether browsing our online collection or visiting our charming Texas hill country storefront, you will discover stunning styles perfect for popping the question, exchanging vows, or celebrating enduring love.

Owner and lead designer Mariana Gallo handcrafts every piece with care and personal attention. After graduating top of her class from the Diamond Exchange Institute, Mariana returned home to Austin to establish her own jewelry brand rooted in quality craftsmanship and sentimental symbolism. For over a decade, Mariana has built Mangata & Gallo into a recognizable name in romantic jewelry, earning accolades for her signature diamond cuts and custom engagement ring settings.

At Mangata & Gallo, we believe finding the perfect ring should feel as special as the "I do." Contact us today to schedule a private consultation with our team of gemologists and designers. We look forward to helping you celebrate life's greatest love stories.
